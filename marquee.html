Birds play a crucial part in a healthy ecosystem. Some are vital because they pollinate plants fertilizing blooms and allowing them to create a new seed. They eat seeds and excrete them out, allowing plants to spread to new regions.

The inner two whorls are androecium or the male part of the flower and gynoecium or the female part of the flower.
